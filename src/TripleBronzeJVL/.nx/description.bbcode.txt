[b][size=4]Triple Bronze JVL[/size][/b]
Triples the amount of bronze you get with each craft so that crafting bronze is consistent (1 tin ingot + 2 copper ingots = 3 bronze ingots) - LolmanXDXD

[size=4][b]Configuration [/b][/size][/size][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3][b]Bronze Multiplier:[/b] The normal recipe result for bronze is multiplied by this value.[/size]
[*][size=3][b]Craft Bars In Forge: [/b]Allows bypassing the smelter by creating crafting recipes for ores/scraps -> bars for the forge after upgrading the forge to the proper tier. [/size]
[*][size=3][b]Coal Per Bar:[/b] Controls how much coal is needed to craft a bar of metal. [/size]
[/list]
